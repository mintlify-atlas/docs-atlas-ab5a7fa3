Study Sync's notification system keeps you informed about your progress, upcoming deadlines, and important updates without being overwhelming.

## Notification System

Stay connected to your learning journey:

<Info>
Study Sync sends notifications via email and in-app alerts to help you stay on track with your study goals.
</Info>

### Notification Types

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Study Reminders">
    ### Daily Study Reminders
    
    Automated reminders to maintain consistent study habits:
    
    **What They Include:**
    - Active instances with incomplete resources
    - Progress summary for each instance
    - Remaining time until deadlines
    - Suggested daily study time
    
    **When They're Sent:**
    - At your configured reminder time (default: 9:00 AM)
    - Based on your timezone
    - Only on days you've selected (default: weekdays)
    - Pauses when all instances are complete
    
    **Customization:**
    - Set preferred time of day
    - Choose which days to receive reminders
    - Enable/disable per instance
    - Snooze temporarily
  </Tab>
  
  <Tab title="Deadline Warnings">
    ### Deadline Warnings
    
    Proactive alerts when deadlines approach:
    
    **Warning Schedule:**
    - **7 days before:** Gentle reminder
    - **3 days before:** Moderate urgency
    - **1 day before:** High priority alert
    - **On deadline day:** Final reminder
    
    **Alert Contents:**
    - Instance name and course code
    - Deadline date and time remaining
    - Current progress percentage
    - Estimated time needed to complete
    - Recommended daily study time
    
    **Smart Warnings:**
    - Adjusts urgency based on progress
    - Suggests acceleration if behind schedule
    - Celebrates if you're ahead
    - Stops after deadline passes
  </Tab>
  
  <Tab title="Progress Updates">
    ### Progress Milestones
    
    Celebrate achievements and maintain motivation:
    
    **Milestone Triggers:**
    - 25% completion
    - 50% completion (halfway!)
    - 75% completion
    - 100% completion (congratulations!)
    
    **What You See:**
    - Instance name and progress
    - Resources completed count
    - Time invested
    - Encouragement message
    - Next steps suggestion
    
    **Motivational Features:**
    - Positive reinforcement
    - Progress visualization
    - Achievement recognition
    - Streak tracking (coming soon)
  </Tab>
  
  <Tab title="Collaboration Alerts">
    ### Collaboration Notifications
    
    Stay informed about shared study plans:
    
    **Share Invitations:**
    - When someone shares a plan with you
    - Your role (viewer or editor)
    - Plan details and resource count
    - Link to view the plan
    
    **Plan Updates:**
    - When editors add new resources (optional)
    - Major changes to shared plans
    - Plan visibility changes
    - Access revocation notices
    
    **Group Activity:**
    - New instances created from your plan
    - Milestones hit by study group (optional)
    - Comments on shared resources (coming soon)
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

## Notification Settings

Customize your notification preferences to match your study style:

### Accessing Settings

1. Click your profile icon in the navigation bar
2. Select "Notification Settings"
3. Configure your preferences
4. Click "Save Changes"

### Email Reminders

<Steps>
  <Step title="Enable/Disable">
    Toggle email reminders on or off globally. When off, you'll still see in-app notifications.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Reminder Time">
    Set your preferred time of day to receive reminders:
    
    - Choose any time in 24-hour format
    - Default: 09:00 (9:00 AM)
    - Based on your account timezone
    - Applies to all daily reminders
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Reminder Frequency">
    Select how often you want reminders:
    
    <Accordion title="Frequency Options">
      - **Daily:** Every single day
      - **Weekdays:** Monday through Friday only
      - **Weekends:** Saturday and Sunday only
      - **Custom Days:** Select specific days of the week
    </Accordion>
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Custom Days">
    If using "Custom Days" frequency, select which days:
    
    - Check boxes for each desired day
    - Monday through Sunday
    - Great for accommodating class schedules
    - Minimum 1 day, maximum 7 days
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Deadline Warnings

<Accordion title="Deadline Warning Settings">
  - **Enable/Disable:** Turn deadline warnings on/off
  - **Warning Schedule:** Choose when to be warned (7, 3, 1 days)
  - **Urgency Level:** Set how insistent warnings should be
  - **Override Per Instance:** Configure differently for specific instances
</Accordion>

### Weekly Digest

Receive a comprehensive weekly summary:

**What's Included:**
- All active instances and their progress
- Upcoming deadlines for the week
- Resources completed this week
- Time spent studying
- Achievements and milestones
- Suggested focus areas

**Configuration:**
- Enable/disable weekly digest
- Choose delivery day (default: Sunday evening)
- Set preferred time
- Select content sections to include

### Custom Reminders

Create one-time or recurring custom reminders:

**Use Cases:**
- Exam date reminders
- Office hours notifications
- Study group meeting alerts
- Project milestone deadlines
- Review session reminders

**Setup:**
1. Click "Add Custom Reminder"
2. Enter reminder text
3. Set date and time
4. Choose recurrence (one-time, daily, weekly)
5. Link to instance (optional)
6. Save reminder

## Notification Best Practices

### Finding Your Balance

- **Start Conservative:** Enable fewer notifications initially
- **Add Gradually:** Increase as you understand your needs
- **Review Regularly:** Adjust settings based on what's helpful
- **Avoid Fatigue:** Too many notifications reduce effectiveness

### Effective Reminder Times

- **Morning Reminders (7-9 AM):** Plan your study day
- **Lunch Reminders (12-1 PM):** Midday motivation check
- **Evening Reminders (6-8 PM):** Post-work study session
- **Night Reminders (9-10 PM):** Before-bed review

**Choose Based On:**
- When you typically study
- Your daily schedule
- Peak productivity hours
- Other commitments

### Managing Email Volume

<Accordion title="Tips to Reduce Email Overload">
  - Use in-app notifications for most alerts
  - Enable email only for critical items (deadlines)
  - Rely on weekly digest instead of daily emails
  - Create email filters for Study Sync notifications
  - Set custom days to match your active study days
</Accordion>

### Leveraging Notifications

**Build Habits:**
- Set consistent daily reminder times
- Use reminders to establish study routines
- Let notifications trigger study sessions

**Stay Accountable:**
- Enable deadline warnings to avoid procrastination
- Share progress with study groups
- Use milestones for motivation

**Reduce Stress:**
- Configure early deadline warnings
- Get ahead with proactive reminders
- Celebrate small wins with milestone notifications

## Privacy and Control

### Unsubscribe Options

Full control over email notifications:

- **Global Unsubscribe:** Turn off all promotional emails
- **Selective Disable:** Turn off specific notification types
- **Per-Instance Control:** Configure notifications per instance
- **One-Click Unsubscribe:** Link in every email footer

### Data Privacy

- Notification data stored securely
- Emails sent via trusted providers
- No sharing of notification preferences
- Delete notification history anytime
- Privacy-first notification system

### Notification Retention

- In-app notifications: Kept for 30 days
- Email notifications: Stored per your email provider's policy
- Custom reminders: Until you delete them
- Settings: Saved indefinitely until you change them

## Troubleshooting

### Not Receiving Emails?

<Steps>
  <Step title="Check Spam Folder">
    Study Sync emails might be filtered as spam. Mark as "Not Spam" to whitelist.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Verify Email Address">
    Ensure your account email is correct in profile settings.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Check Settings">
    Confirm email notifications are enabled in notification settings.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Whitelist Domain">
    Add @studysync.com to your email whitelist.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Contact Support">
    If issues persist, reach out to support for assistance.
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Missing In-App Notifications?

- **Refresh the page:** Notifications load on page load
- **Check browser permissions:** Allow notifications in browser settings
- **Clear cache:** Browser cache might be stale
- **Try incognito:** Test if extensions are interfering

### Wrong Timezone?

- Notifications use your account timezone
- Update timezone in profile settings
- Changes apply to future notifications
- May take up to 24 hours to reflect
