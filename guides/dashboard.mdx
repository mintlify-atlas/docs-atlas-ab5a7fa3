Your dashboard is mission control for your learning journey. It provides a centralized view of your progress, upcoming deadlines, and active study plans.

## Dashboard Overview

The dashboard is organized into several key sections designed to help you stay on track and resume your studies quickly.

## Continue Learning

The most prominent section shows your **most recently accessed instance**.

### What You'll See

- **Most Recent Badge**: Indicates this was your last accessed plan
- **Last Accessed Time**: Shows when you last worked on this (e.g., "2 hours ago")
- **Course & Title**: Full identification of the study plan
- **Description**: Brief reminder of what the plan covers
- **Progress Stats**:
  - Resources completed (e.g., "7/15 Resources")
  - Time spent (e.g., "3h spent")
  - Progress percentage with visual bar
- **Resume Button**: Click to jump directly back into your studies

<Tip>
  The Continue Learning section remembers your exact position, so you can pick up exactly where you left off.
</Tip>

## Upcoming Deadlines

Stay on top of your commitments with the deadlines panel.

### Features

- **Top 3 Deadlines**: Shows your three soonest due dates
- **Days Until Due**: Quick countdown ("Today", "2 days", etc.)
- **Color Coding**:
  - Red: Due today or in 1-2 days (urgent)
  - Orange: Due in 3+ days (upcoming)
- **Due Date**: Full calendar date for reference
- **Direct Links**: Click any deadline to open that instance

### No Deadlines State

If you have no upcoming deadlines:
- Shows a green checkmark
- "No upcoming deadlines" message
- Gives you peace of mind

<Tip>
  Set realistic deadlines when starting instances. You can always adjust them later by editing the instance details.
</Tip>

## Your Courses

A grid view of all your other active study plan instances (beyond your most recent).

### Grid Card Information

- **Course Code & Title**: Identify each instance
- **Resource Count**: e.g., "5/12 Resources"
- **Progress Percentage**: Shows how far along you are
- **Progress Bar**: Visual indicator of completion
- **Hover Effects**: Cards highlight when you hover over them

### Viewing More

- Grid shows up to 6 instances
- Click **View All** to see your complete list
- Sorted by last access date (most recent first)

## Quick Actions

Convenient shortcuts at the bottom of your dashboard:

- **Browse All Plans**: Discover new public study plans
- **My Created Plans**: View and manage plans you've created

## Empty State (No Instances)

If you haven't started any study plans yet, you'll see:

- Welcome message
- Explanation of how to get started
- Two prominent buttons:
  - **Browse Plans**: Explore public plans
  - **Create Plan**: Build your own

<Tip>
  New to Study Sync? Start by browsing public plans related to your current courses. You can always create custom plans later.
</Tip>

## Header Section

### Welcome Message

- Displays "Welcome back, [Your Name]"
- Uses your display name from profile
- Personalizes the experience

### Create New Plan Button

Always accessible in the top-right corner:
- Click to start creating a new study plan
- Available regardless of scroll position
- Quick access to plan creation

## Understanding Progress Indicators

### Progress Percentage

Calculated as:
```
Progress % = (Completed Resources / Total Resources) × 100
```

**Important**: Progress is based on resource count, not time. A 5-minute video counts the same as a 2-hour video.

### Time Tracking

Two time metrics appear:

1. **Time Spent**: Sum of durations for all completed resources
2. **Remaining Time**: Sum of durations for incomplete resources

<Note>
  Time estimates are based on resource metadata (video length, PDF page count, etc.). Your actual time may vary.
</Note>

## Navigating From Dashboard

<Steps>
  <Step title="Resume Study Session">
    Click the **Resume** button in the Continue Learning section to jump directly into your most recent instance.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Check on Deadline">
    Click any item in Upcoming Deadlines to open that specific instance.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Switch Courses">
    Click any course card in Your Courses to switch to a different study plan.
  </Step>

  <Step title="View All Instances">
    Click **View All** next to Your Courses, or use the main navigation to go to the Instances page.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Start New Plan">
    Click **Create New Plan** in the header, or use Quick Actions to browse existing plans.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Dashboard Best Practices

<Tip>
  **Daily Check-In**: Visit your dashboard daily to see deadlines and resume where you left off.
</Tip>

<Tip>
  **Priority Focus**: Tackle items in the Upcoming Deadlines section first.
</Tip>

<Tip>
  **Balance Multiple Plans**: Avoid starting too many instances at once. Focus on 2-3 active plans for best results.
</Tip>

<Tip>
  **Update Regularly**: If circumstances change, adjust deadlines rather than abandoning instances.
</Tip>

## Making the Most of Your Dashboard

### Morning Routine

1. Open Study Sync dashboard
2. Check Upcoming Deadlines
3. Review progress on current instance
4. Set a goal for the day (e.g., "Complete 3 resources")
5. Click Resume and start studying

### Weekly Review

1. View all instances to see overall progress
2. Identify any falling-behind plans
3. Adjust deadlines if needed
4. Celebrate completed plans
5. Start new instances for next week's topics

### Month-End Review

1. Review all completed instances from the month
2. Revisit notes from completed resources
3. Assess which resource types worked best for you
4. Plan next month's learning goals
5. Create or find new study plans

## Visual Indicators

### Progress Bars

All progress bars use a gradient color scheme:
- Blue to purple gradient
- Fills left to right
- Smooth animation when updated
- Matches your progress percentage

### Status Badges

- **Most Recent**: Blue badge on your last accessed instance
- **Shared**: Blue badge on plans shared with you
- **Public**: Green badge on public plans
- **Urgent**: Red badge on imminent deadlines

### Icons

- **PlayCircle**: Continue Learning section

- **Desktop**: Three-column layout for optimal viewing

</Note>

## Troubleshooting

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Dashboard not showing recent instance">
    - Try refreshing the page
    - Ensure you're logged in
    - Check if you have any active instances
    - Clear browser cache if issues persist
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Progress percentage seems wrong">
    - Progress is based on resource count, not time
    - Verify completion status of each resource in the instance
    - Some resources may have been added/removed in edit mode
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Deadline not showing up">
    - Only instances with set deadlines appear
    - Only future deadlines are shown (not past due)
    - Maximum of 3 deadlines displayed
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Instance missing from Your Courses">
    - The most recent instance appears in Continue Learning instead
    - Scroll down or click View All to see all instances
    - Check if you're on the correct account
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
