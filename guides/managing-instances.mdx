Study instances are your personal progress trackers for study plans. Each instance represents your journey through a specific study plan with deadline tracking and resource completion status.

## What is a Study Instance?

A study instance is a personalized copy of a study plan that tracks:
- Which resources you've completed
- Your overall progress percentage
- Time spent and time remaining
- Personal notes for each resource
- Target completion date

You can create multiple instances of the same study plan (for example, if you're retaking a course).

## Starting a New Instance

<Steps>
  <Step title="Find a Study Plan">
    Browse available plans from:
    - **Browse Plans** page for public plans
    - **My Plans** page for plans you created or have access to
    - Search by course code or title
  </Step>

  <Step title="Review Plan Details">
    Click on a plan to view:
    - Full resource list
    - Total estimated time
    - Course description
    - Creator information
  </Step>

  <Step title="Click Start This Plan">
    On the plan details page, click the green **Start This Plan** button.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Set Your Deadline">
    Choose a target completion date:
    - Must be in the future
    - Consider the total time estimate
    - Default suggestion is 7 days from today
    - You can modify this later if needed
  </Step>

  <Step title="Begin Learning">
    After confirming, you'll be taken to your instance where you can start working through resources immediately.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Working Through Resources

Once inside an instance, you'll see a YouTube-style interface:

### Main Player Area

- **Video/Resource Display**: Embedded player for YouTube videos and preview for other resources
- **Resource Information**: Title, type, duration, and completion status
- **Action Buttons**:
  - **Mark Complete**: Toggle completion status
  - **Take Notes**: Add personal notes for the resource
  - **Open in New Tab**: Access the resource externally

### Playlist Sidebar

- **Progress Overview**: See completed count and remaining time
- **Resource List**: All resources with:
  - Completion checkmarks
  - Duration indicators
  - Note indicators (yellow sticky note icon)
  - Currently playing indicator
- **Quick Navigation**: Click any resource to jump to it

### Theater Mode

For distraction-free learning:
- Click **Theater Mode** button
- Expands the player to full width
- Playlist moves below the player
- Press 'T' keyboard shortcut to toggle

## Tracking Your Progress

### Progress Bar

At the top of every instance:
- Visual progress bar showing completion percentage
- Updates in real-time as you mark resources complete
- Based on number of resources completed (not time)

### Dashboard Overview

Your dashboard shows:
- **Continue Learning**: Your most recently accessed instance
- **Upcoming Deadlines**: Instances with approaching due dates
- **Your Courses**: Grid of all active instances with progress

### Completion Tracking

Mark resources complete as you finish them:
1. Complete the resource (watch video, read article, etc.)
2. Click **Mark Complete** button
3. The resource gets a green checkmark
4. Progress percentage updates automatically
5. Completed resources show in strikethrough in the playlist

## Taking Notes

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Notes Panel">
    Click the yellow **sticky note icon** or **Take Notes** button for any resource.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Write Your Notes">
    Type your thoughts, key takeaways, or questions in the text area.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Auto-Save">
    Notes are automatically saved after you stop typing. Watch for the "Saving..." indicator.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
  Notes are synced across devices and saved to your account. You can access them anytime you return to the instance.
</Tip>

## Editing Your Instance

Customize your instance to fit your needs:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Enter Edit Mode">
    Click the blue **Edit** button in the top bar of your instance.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Make Changes">
    In edit mode, you can:
    
    - **Reorder Resources**: Drag the grip handles to rearrange
    - **Rename Resources**: Click on any title to edit it
    - **Remove Resources**: Click the trash icon to delete
    - **Add Resources**: Click the **Add** button to insert new materials
  </Step>

  <Step title="Save Changes">
    Click the green **Save** button to apply your changes, or **Cancel** to discard them.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Warning>
  Changes to an instance only affect YOUR copy. They don't modify the original study plan or other people's instances.
</Warning>

## Adding Resources to an Instance

While in edit mode:

1. Click the **Add** button in the playlist header
2. Select the resource type
3. Enter the URL and any required information
4. Click **Add Resource**
5. The resource appears at the bottom of your list
6. Drag it to the desired position
7. Click **Save** to confirm

## Managing Multiple Instances

### Viewing All Instances

From the dashboard or instances page, you can:
- See all your active study instances
- Filter by course code
- Sort by progress, deadline, or last accessed
- Quickly resume any instance

### Naming Instances

By default, instances use the study plan title. You can add a custom title to differentiate multiple instances of the same plan (for example, "CSE101 - Midterm Prep" vs "CSE101 - Final Review").

## Best Practices

<Tip>
  **Set Realistic Deadlines**: Consider the total time estimate and your daily availability. It's better to finish early than to feel rushed.
</Tip>

<Tip>
  **Daily Study Habit**: Access your instance daily, even for just 15-30 minutes. Consistent progress is key.
</Tip>

<Tip>
  **Use Notes Effectively**: Write notes as you go, not later. Capture key insights, questions, and connections to other topics.
</Tip>

<Tip>
  **Mark Complete Honestly**: Only mark resources complete when you truly understand them. You can always uncheck and review again.
</Tip>

## Completing an Instance

When you mark the final resource as complete:
- A congratulations message appears
- Your progress shows 100%
- The instance remains accessible for review
- You can still access all notes and resources

<Check>
  Congratulations on completing your study plan! You can start a new instance anytime to refresh your knowledge.
</Check>
